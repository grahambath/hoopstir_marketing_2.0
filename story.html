The Hoopstir Story

Est. 2018

Built for Basketball...

October 2018 - Hoopstir 1.0 successfully launches in Canda. With the early adoption and enthusiasm from local organizations Hoopstir begins it's journey as the only registration platform built exclusively for basketball.



Website Design and Development

While continuing to add more features while reaching more clients Hoopstir expands its service offerings to include website design, development, and management.

Hoopstir Media, at your service.

In collaboration with KB Kutz, Hoopstir begins to capture and curate digital content helping users tell their stories and showcase their basketball organizations.

Built For Basketball... In North America

After 2 years of adding features and perfecting the registration process we welcome the Carlos Boozer Basketball Camp into the Hoopstir family and officially enter the USA.

Changing the way we teach and learn.

In March 2020 the world is gripped by the Coronavirus bringing youth baskebtall to a sudden halt. From the grassroots to the pros, every level of basketball is affected. Hoopstir's virtual training platform launches providing a virtual space for basketball lessons and training.
