About Us


Get buckets

Hoopstir is a Sports Software application designed to support and automate administrative tasks required for basketball events. Hoopstir saves administrators time and effort, makes paying fees and buying merchandise a snap, promotes effective communication between organizations and participants, and stores athlete data in a structured manner that allows it to be reviewed and communicated.

Unlike competing platforms, Hoopstir treats basketball as a unique discipline and provides discipline-specific administrative and coaching solutions.
